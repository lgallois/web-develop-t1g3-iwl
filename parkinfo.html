Parking & Directions

Parking Fees:
Standard - $25 per night, Premium - $35 per night
Public Transport Access:
Direct train station access via the W and D lines from Central Station.
Shuttle Bus Services:
Daily shuttle services right to the park gates at 20 minute intervals between 8am to 4pm. Queue for the shuttle service can be located right outside VivoCity Mall's exit G.

Safety Information

COVID-19 Measures:
Face coverings are recommended for individuals who have not received a COVID-19 vaccine.
Age Requirements:
All children above the age of 12 are welcome on our rides. KiddieLand is available to entertain all children under 12.
First-Aid Stations & Clinics:
There are a total of 5 first-aid stations and 2 clinics spread around the park, offering basic injury care and diagnosis.
