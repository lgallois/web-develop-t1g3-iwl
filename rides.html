Rides & Attractions

Single/Dual Rides

Buckle up!

Single/dual rides allow groups to split up and embark on selected attractions individually or in pair! Single/dual riders enjoy shorter queue lines and reduced wait time to make the most of their day at T12!

Group Rides

Best value tickets for groups

Seize the day with our discounted tickets for groups of 10+!
At T12, you'll find many attractions able to accomodate large parties of passengers, at a special price!

Family Rides

Fun for all ages

From exciting attractions to fairytale shows, our park features many rides for the little ones as well.
A wide range of services for parents are also available, so that the whole family can enjoy a magical and peaceful visit in T12!

Thriller/Rollercoaster

Adrenaline peak

Do you prefer a spine-tingling, heart-pumping experience?
